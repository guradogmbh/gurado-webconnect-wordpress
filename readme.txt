== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= The widget does not render =
Ensure `shop` is set and the vendor script is reachable over HTTPS.

= Do I need a payment provider? =
You can connect your existing provider (PayPal, Mollie, Stripe, etc.) or choose from over 10 integrated PSPs directly in your gurado dashboard.

= Can I sell services and event tickets, not just gift cards? =
Yes. gurado supports money-value vouchers, service-based vouchers, configurable packages, and even event ticketing with recurring schedules.

= Does gurado take a commission per sale? =
No. gurado takes no commission. You only pay the standard fees from your chosen payment provider.

= Can customers redeem gift cards in-store? =
Yes. gurado integrates with 23 EPOS systems including Bizerba, Matrix POS, Melzer X3000, Novacom, Untill, and TCPOS.
